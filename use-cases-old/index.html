IVRS
Interactive Voice Response (IVR) is an automated telephony system that interacts with callers, gathers information and routes calls to the appropriate recipient.
Get Started with IVRS
Voice OTP
Voice OTP is a call with a secret code that enables you to verify new user registrations in an app or website. Think of it as SMS OTP, but with voice.
Get Started with Voice OTP
Voice Alerts & Broadcasting
A voice alert is a call that provides users with timely information. With Plivo's API, you can send voice alerts or notifications to your users from your web or mobile application.
Get Started with Voice Alerts
Number Masking
Connect two or more people on a call without revealing their original phone numbers. You can seamlessly facilitate interaction in both incoming and outgoing calls using our API and Plivo XML.
Get Started with Number Masking
Call Tracking
Call tracking is a method of tracking conversions and measuring ROI on marketing campaigns using the analytics of inbound calls from customers.
